BapBot v1.0.0 | 9/15/2024 

*****Initial Release*****

Initial launch available commands: 
    
    Introducing basic general commands.
    - ❓'./help' Displays all commands.
    - 📃'./changelog {version}' Displays provided versions changelog otherwise displays all versions.
    - 🔗'./invite' Provides invite link for bot. 

Other:

    Bot activity will change every 30 minutes. 
    Every 10 messages sent, the bot will send a random cat or frog gif. (amt. of messages sent subject to change)
    Every 15 messages sent, the bot will say a random string of words gathered from all text channels. (amt. of messages sent subject to chanage)

Next patch commands: 

    'Fun' commands. 
    - ❌⭕'./ttt' as tic-tac-toe. Player vs. Player or Player vs. Bot  
    - 🐟'./fish'. Catch fish of different rarities. (Varieties of different fish and rarities added every update.)
    - 🪨📃✂️'./rps' as rock-paper-scissors. Player vs Player. 
    - 🪙'./coinflip' Basic coinflip command.  
    - 👁️'./stalk'. See what {given_username} is doing on a specific platform. Works with Roblox. Will work with STEAM API soon.
    - 👑'./lb'. See the server's leaderboard stats in different activities. Fish collection, rock-paper-scissors wins, tic-tac-toe wins, etc.
